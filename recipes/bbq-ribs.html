BBQ RIBS RECIPE

Michael's BBQ RIBS RECIPE

DESCRIPTION

The best way to make ribs at home!

Ingredients

  • rack of ribs
  • brown sugar
  • salt
  • pepper
  • garlic powder
  • paprika
  • cinnamon
  • chipotle pepper
  • oil
  • worcestershire sauce
  • butter
  • tin foil
  • favorite bbq sauce

Steps

  1. Remove the silver skin on the back of the ribs
  2. Create dry rub
    • 1/4 cup brown sugar
    • 2 tsp salt
    • 2 tsp pepper
    • 2 tsp garlic powder
    • 2 tsp paprika
    • 1/2 tsp cinnamon
    • 1/4 tsp chipotle pepper
  3. Apply oil on the ribs
  4. Lather the dry rub over the ribs
  5. Apply worcestershire sauce
  6. Tightly cover in tin foil
  7. Bake for 2.5 hours at 275 degrees
  8. Add butter and cook another 30 mins
  9. Apply favorite bbq sauce and broil for 10 minutes
