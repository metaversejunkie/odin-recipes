Banana Bread Recipe

Moist Banana Bread Recipe

Make the best banana bread!

Ingredients

  • brown sugar
  • butter
  • greek yogurt
  • eggs
  • flour
  • baking soda
  • bananas
  • chocolate chips
  • loaf pan

Steps

  1. Mix following ingredients until no lumps
    • 1 cup brown sugar
    • 1/4 cup butter (room temperature)
    • 1/3 cup greek yogurt
  2. Add 2 eggs to mixture and mix together
  3. 1/2 cups of flour and mix
  4. 1/4 tsp of baking soda and mix
  5. Mash up 3 ripe bananas and add to mixture
  6. 1/2 cup of semi sweet chocolate chips
  7. Grease loaf pan
  8. Sprinkle brown sugar to the bottom of the pan
  9. Throw batter into greased loaf pan
  10. Top with more chocolate chips
  11. Slam pan on counter to get rid of air bubbles
  12. Bake at 350 degrees for about an hour
