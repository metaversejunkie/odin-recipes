Yellow Rice Recipe

Yellow Rice

Description

How to make gorgeous yellow rice at home

Ingredients

  • rice (Recommend Basmati or long grain)
  • chicken broth
  • garlic powder
  • tumeric
  • paprika
  • onion powder
  • salt
  • pepper
  • olive oil
  • rice cooker

Steps

  1. 2 cups rice (washed and drained)
  2. place rice in rice cooker
  3. add 2 1/4 cups chicken broth
  4. add 1 tbsp olive oil
  5. add 1 tsp turmeric
  6. add 1 tsp paprika
  7. add 1 tsp onion powder
  8. add 1 tsp garlic powder
  9. add 1 tsp salt
  10. add 1 tsp pepper
  11. start rice cooker
